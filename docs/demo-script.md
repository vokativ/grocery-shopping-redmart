# 45–90 second demo script

## Recording checklist

- Use a clean demo account or sanitize all names, addresses, order numbers, prices, tabs, notifications, and account menus.
- Never show cookies, developer storage, payment details, or checkout screens.
- Use captions large enough for a phone screen.
- Record the real workflow; do not imply support that has not been tested.

## Sequence

1. **The burden (5–10s):** show the familiar whiteboard or dictated list. Caption: “We knew what we wanted. Finding it all again was the work.”
2. **Known preferences (10–15s):** show several family words mapping to saved products and quantities. Caption: “No random recommendations—our household's usual products.”
3. **Approval (10s):** show the proposed cart before browser actions.
4. **Execution (15–25s):** show one availability check and one quantity update in RedMart.
5. **Verification (10–15s):** show the actual cart rows and quantities being checked.
6. **Human boundary (5–10s):** leave the cart open. Caption: “Delivery, payment, and checkout stay with us.”
7. **Closing:** “The family makes the decisions once. The agent handles the repetition. Free and open source.”
